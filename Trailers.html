Our Trailers

What are Trailers?

A trailer is basically a container in which you can carry
personal, commercial or industrial loads. They aren't just rectangular
boxes, they could be open too for transporting industrial tools.

Types Of Trailers

There are several types of trailers available in the market.
But as we deal only with Industrial loads, we have only 53 feet trailers.

Van

These are 53 feet long containers that can carry weight upto 45,000 pounds.

Van With Team

These are 53 feet long containers that can carry weight upto 45,000 pounds. But in this type, drivers have a companion with them as the journey is long

Reefer

These are 53 feet long refrigerated containers that can go to temperatures as low as -15°C carry weight upto 42,000 pounds.
